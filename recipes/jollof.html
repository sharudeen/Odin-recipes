Jollof

Jollof or jollof rice, is a rice dish from West Africa. The rice is cook in tomatoes sours with other vegetables all in a single pot.

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 2 large onion, diced
  • 5 bonnet chile pepper, blended
  • 3 large tomatoes, diced
  • 1 teblespoon tomato paste
  • 200 grams of smoked mackerel or 400 grams canned mackerel
  • 1 chicken bouillon tablet
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 Cups of long grain Jazmin Rice

Steps

  1. Heat 3 tablespoons vegetable oil in a large pot over medium-low heat.
  2. Add and cook 2 diced onion until soft and translucent but not browned, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add blended pepper and cook for a minute or two.
  4. Add chopped tomatoes and the tomato paste with a little water; cook and stir until slightly thickened and infused into the oil, 5 to 7 minutes.
  5. Add the bouillon tablet and the mackerel and cook on low heat until oil begins to surface.
  6. Add washed rice, stir and add equal part water.
  7. Add salt to taste, cover and cook on low heat until it's done.
  8. Serve with any grilled/boil/baked protien, also with salad (all optional).
